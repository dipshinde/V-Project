## Key Design Decisions

### Why NO Button Dodges
- Creates playful moment
- Builds anticipation
- Memorable interaction
- Eventually disappears (not frustrating)
- YES button growth adds comedy

### Why Name Gate
- Immediate personalization
- Sets intimate tone
- Creates moment of "this is for me"
- Smooth transition to hero

### Why Timeline Before Gallery
- Text-first, image-second
- Builds emotional context
- Photos have more impact after story
- Better pacing

### Why Letter Last
- Saves best for last
- Emotional climax
- Envelope animation adds ritual
- Leaves lasting impression
