## Writing Tips

### For Timeline
1. Start with when, not what
2. Include a feeling, not just facts
3. Use "uss din" (that day) to create nostalgia
4. Mix specific details with emotions
5. Keep it under 2 sentences

### For Gallery Captions
1. Make it feel spontaneous
2. Can be playful or romantic
3. Inside jokes work great
4. One emotion per caption
5. Use emojis naturally

### For Love Letter
1. Start personal ("Hey [name]")
2. Include specific memories
3. Mix languages where it feels right
4. Don't overthink - write how you talk
5. End with commitment/promise
6. Sign off personally

### General Rules
âœ… Be specific: "that coffee shop" not "a place"
âœ… Use real details: dates, locations, moments
âœ… Write like you talk to her
âœ… Emotion > Poetry
âœ… Genuine > Perfect

## Personalization Checklist

When customizing, ask yourself:

**For Timeline:**
- [ ] Are these OUR actual dates?
- [ ] Would she remember these moments?
- [ ] Do the descriptions sound like me?
- [ ] Did I include why each moment mattered?

**For Gallery:**
- [ ] Are these photos meaningful to both of us?
- [ ] Do captions add context or emotion?
- [ ] Would she smile reading these?
- [ ] Any inside jokes she'll recognize?

**For Letter:**
- [ ] Does this sound like something I would say?
- [ ] Did I include specific examples?
- [ ] Is it honest about my feelings?
- [ ] Does it feel personal to our relationship?

**For Date Ideas:**
- [ ] Would we actually enjoy these?
- [ ] Are they realistic for our situation?
- [ ] Mix of her favorites and new ideas?
- [ ] No virtual/LDR references?
