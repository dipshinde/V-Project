### Required Photos:
- `photo1.jpg` - First special photo
- `photo2.jpg` - Second special photo
- `photo3.jpg` - Third special photo
- `photo4.jpg` - Fourth special photo

### Optional Photos:
- `photo5.jpg`, `photo6.jpg`, etc. - Add as many as you want!

### Recommended Specs:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 1200x900px (or similar ratio)
- **File size**: Under 2MB each
- **Quality**: High resolution, good lighting

### Where They'll Appear:
Gallery section with your custom captions from `js/config.js`

### Tips:
- Choose photos that tell your story
- Variety of moments (dates, candid, special occasions)
- Good quality (not blurry or pixelated)
- Mix of close-ups and full shots
- Photos that make you smile
